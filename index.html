About Me

I'm a Quantitative Finance student at NUS (GPA 4.81/5.0, Dean's List 2026), studying at the intersection of stochastic processes, portfolio theory, and machine learning — with a double major in Business Analytics and a minor in Data Science.

My work spans quant research internships at hedge fund startups, investment analysis at family offices, and competitive trading hackathons. I'm drawn to problems where rigorous mathematics meets practical market intuition — from vol surface modelling to NLP-driven signal generation.

I published mathematical research in Q1/Q2 academic journals and am currently working on "Multi-Asset Volatility Coupling via Shared Latent Space". Outside of markets I enjoy solving puzzles like sudoku and crosswords, as well as cooking.
